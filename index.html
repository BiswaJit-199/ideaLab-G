What is an IDEA Lab?

An AICTE-recognized IDEA Lab is a multidisciplinary innovation space where students transform ideas into impactful solutions through hands-on learning, prototyping, and real-world application.

01

Ideation

Identify real-world problems using design thinking and creativity.

02

Prototyping

Convert ideas into functional prototypes using advanced tools.

03

Application

Scale innovations into startups, patents, and deployable solutions.

Facilities & Infrastructure

State-of-the-art labs empowering innovation from concept to realization.

Additive Manufacturing Lab

Industrial-grade 3D printing for rapid prototyping and complex part fabrication.

Digital Manufacturing Lab

CNC machining, laser cutting, and digitally controlled fabrication systems.

IoT & Automation Lab

Smart systems development using sensors, controllers, and automation hardware.

Chip / PCB Design Lab

Circuit design, PCB layout, prototyping, and electronic hardware testing.

Product Design Lab

Human-centered product ideation, ergonomics, and design thinking workflows.

Design & Simulation Studio

CAD, CAE, and simulation tools for design validation and optimization.

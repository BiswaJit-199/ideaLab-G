What is an IDEA Lab?

An AICTE-recognized IDEA Lab is a multidisciplinary innovation space where students transform ideas into impactful solutions through hands-on learning, prototyping, and real-world application.

01

Ideation

Identify real-world problems using design thinking and creativity.

02

Prototyping

Convert ideas into functional prototypes using advanced tools.

03

Application

Scale innovations into startups, patents, and deployable solutions.
